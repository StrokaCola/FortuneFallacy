Celestial Dice — Live Physics
Five dice in a felt-padded tray. Click Roll to toss them, or click any die in the scene to flick it. Rapier handles tumbling and resting; readFace() projects each canonical face normal through the body's quaternion to determine which face landed up. All visual + collider + face-detection logic lives in physics-die.js.
